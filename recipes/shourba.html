Shourba

Shourba is a traditional Algerian soup made with vegetables, aromatic spices, and often enriched with proteins like chicken or lamb.

Ingedients:
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 potato, diced
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • Fresh cilantro or parsley for garnish

Steps:
  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the pot. Saut√© until the onion becomes translucent and fragrant.
  3. Add the diced carrot, potato, and tomato to the pot. Stir and cook for a few minutes until the vegetables start to soften.
  4. Sprinkle the ground cumin, coriander, paprika, salt, and pepper over the vegetables. Stir well to coat the vegetables with the spices.
  5. Pour the vegetable or chicken broth into the pot. Bring the mixture to a boil.
  6. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let the soup simmer for about 20-25 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.
  7. Once the soup is cooked, use an immersion blender or a regular blender to puree the soup until smooth. Be careful when blending hot liquids.
  8. Taste the soup and adjust the seasoning if needed.
  9. the shourba hot, garnished with fresh cilantro or parsley if desired.
